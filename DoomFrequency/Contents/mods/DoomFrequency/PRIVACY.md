**Effective date:** 2026-04-04
**Controller:** Codex Dev, the Netherlands

---

## 1. Who we are

The DoomFrequency Service is operated by Codex Dev, a business registered in the Netherlands.
When this Policy says "we", "us", or "our", it means Codex Dev.

For data protection enquiries, contact us at root@doomfrequency.fm.

---

## 2. What data we collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the Service:

| Data | Who it concerns | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Email address | Server Owners (registrants) | Account management and subscription |
| Messages sent via the relayer | Server Owner's server | Delivering Service content (roleplay, quests, etc.) |
| IP addresses | Anyone connecting to the API or WebSocket | Security, DDoS protection, and rate-limiting |

To protect player privacy, the Mod applies a one-way SHA-256 hash to player identifiers (such as
Steam IDs) before transmission. This ensures that while we can distinguish between different
radio callers for gameplay purposes, we do not receive or store raw, human-readable identities.
This pseudonymization significantly limits our ability to identify individual players. However,
in accordance with GDPR, pseudonymized data is still considered personal data, and we treat it
accordingly.

---

## 3. Why we collect it — lawful basis

Under GDPR, we rely on the following lawful bases:

- **Email address**: Contract performance — your email is required to create and manage your
  account and subscription. Without it, we cannot provide the Service.
- **Relayed messages**: Legitimate interests — messages are processed in transit to generate and
  deliver the Service's content. Player identifiers (username, Steam ID) are pseudonymized
  client-side using SHA-256 before transmission; Codex Dev never receives the raw values, and our
  ability to identify individual players from the hashed data alone is significantly limited.

---

## 4. How long we keep your data

- **Email address**: Kept for as long as your account is active, plus 30 days after account
  deletion to allow for recovery or dispute resolution. After that, it is permanently deleted.
- **Relayed messages**: Processed transiently. Not stored in a form tied to any individual
  identity beyond what is needed to operate the Service.
- **IP addresses**: Retained in server logs for a maximum of 30 days, after which they are
  automatically deleted.

Notwithstanding the above, data contained within invoices and financial records (such as the
email address associated with a payment) will be retained for 7 years to comply with Dutch
statutory tax record-keeping obligations (*fiscale bewaarplicht*, Article 52 AWR). This legal
obligation takes precedence over any request for erasure that concerns such records.

---

## 5. Automated decision-making

The AI used in the Service is for entertainment and roleplay purposes only. It does not perform
automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects concerning Users or
Server Owners. Article 22 of the GDPR therefore does not apply to the Service's use of AI.

## 6. Your rights under GDPR

If you are based in the EU or EEA, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access**: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Rectification**: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
- **Restriction**: Ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Objection**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at root@doomfrequency.fm. We will respond within
30 days.

If you believe your rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the
Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens) at autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl.

---

## 7. Sub-processors

Under GDPR, we are required to disclose the third-party services we use to process data on our
behalf. We do not sell or rent your data. We share it with sub-processors only to the extent
necessary to operate the Service.

| Sub-processor | Location | Purpose | Data involved |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Stripe** | Ireland / USA | Payment processing and subscription management | Email address, invoices, subscription status |
| **[AI Provider]** | USA | Processing relayed game messages to generate AI-driven radio content | Messages sent via the relayer |
| **[Cloudflare]** | USA | Infrastructure hosting, DDoS protection, and content delivery | Traffic metadata |

**Note on AI processing:** Messages sent to the AI provider are used solely for generating
in-game content.

**Note on Stripe:** Codex Dev accesses only your invoices and active subscription status from
Stripe. Your payment card details are collected and stored by Stripe directly and are never
visible to Codex Dev. Stripe operates under its own Privacy Policy at stripe.com/privacy.

When required by law or a binding order from a competent authority in the Netherlands or the EU,
data may also be disclosed to public authorities.

Where data is transferred to providers outside the European Economic Area (EEA), such as the
United States, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with GDPR
Chapter V. This includes reliance on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) adopted by the European
Commission, or on the EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework where the recipient is certified under it.
